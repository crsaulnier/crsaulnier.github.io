Wilderness Engineering

Alcohol Stoves

Designing for the wilderness environment

What if you had to make your own stove?

Something that you had to cook all of your food on for a week? What if I only gave you aluminum cans and denatured alcohol?

Students get to experience design in an impactful way as both a user and designer. You can't just throw it out after getting a grade.

Background Information

In this lesson students will be continuing to apply an engineering science mindset to problems encountered in a wilderness environment.

Connection to Research

Connect to authenticity.

“Design is part of your ability to survive. It’s not just like a far off concept like designing the next big technological thing but it’s in every aspect of my life.”
