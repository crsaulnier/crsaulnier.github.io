Background Information

A wild woozy is a common low ropes course element. It consists of two foot cables that form a V between trees, starting close together and getting progressivly further apart. If you don't have access to a low ropes course - you can create your own with two slacklines and three trees in the right shape.

This activity uses free body diagrams to analyse the forces in the system.
