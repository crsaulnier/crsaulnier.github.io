Systems Engineering

Initially served as the sole electrical and software engineer, taking full responsibility for the design, development, and implementation of electronics and software for early-stage prototypes of spectroscopic instruments for use in the biomanufacturing processes.

As a (very) full stack engineer I was responsible for everything from developing custom high-precision circuit boards, firmware to communicate with custom hardware, robust software systems that could be left unmonitored for experiments lasting up to 6 weeks in length, control systems to control critical parameters of fed batch and perfusion cell cultures, and data acquisition software for recording spectroscopic data and directly feeding that data into chemometric models.

Collaborated directly with customers to understand their needs and specifications, leading to the development of customised prototypes tailored to solve specific challenges.

Conducted on-site experiments at client locations, utilizing these opportunities to gather critical feedback and insights directly from the end users and operational environments.


Engineering Leadership

Built and led the electrical and software engineering teams, later expanding my leadership to also encompass the mechanical engineering team.

Implemented Agile methodologies within the software team, leading to enhanced productivity and efficiency. This success led to the adoption of Agile practices across the entire company, fostering a dynamic and responsive development environment

Wrote proposals for, and oversaw engagements with, product development consulting firms when necessary to leverage external expertise to enhance our product offerings and reduce time-to-market.
